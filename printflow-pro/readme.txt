Gestion complète de votre imprimerie — du devis à la livraison. Complete printing business management platform built on WooCommerce.

== Description ==

PrintFlow Pro transforms WordPress + WooCommerce into a complete printing business management platform. Designed specifically for printing companies in Morocco and French-speaking markets.

**Features:**

* Online print store with dynamic pricing calculator
* Automatic product creation for 20+ print product types
* File upload and artwork management
* Quote request and management system
* Production workflow with kanban board
* Raw materials and inventory management
* Supplier and distributor management
* Full financial tracking (income, expenses, profit)
* CRM with customer loyalty program
* Delivery management and tracking
* Comprehensive reports and dashboards
* Automated notifications and alerts
* French interface for public-facing pages

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `printflow-pro` folder to `/wp-content/plugins/`
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Make sure WooCommerce is installed and activated
4. Follow the setup wizard to configure your printing business
